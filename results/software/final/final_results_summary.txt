5. COMPARISON TO PAPER'S RESULTS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

Paper claims (Sun et al. 2026, Table III):
  ISR improvement: 42.19%
  Semantic accuracy improvement: 29.75%
  Delay reduction: 33.40%

Our results:
  ISR improvement (HDM vs SAC): +15.5%
  Paper: +42.19%
  Gap: 26.6 percentage points

Why the gap:
  1. Our environment is simplified (numpy formulas vs full 3GPP sim)
  2. Training: 500 episodes vs paper's full convergence
  3. Baselines: untrained (random weights) vs paper's trained baselines
  4. Channel: simplified multipath model vs full 3GPP TR 38.901
  5. LLM: Qwen2-VL-7B 4-bit vs paper's LLaVA-NeXT 8-bit

What IS validated:
  + Full pipeline works end-to-end (intent -> LAM -> HDM -> channel -> CSCQI)
  + HDM trained and beats all baselines at n=5
  + CSCQI convergence shows N=6 optimal (matches paper)
  + Ablation shows HAN and DDPM both contribute
  + RAG intent parser with ISREL/ISSUP reflection tokens
  + Source simplification (Algorithm 1) working
  + MIM-based MCS selection working
  + All 3GPP parameters implemented
  + All paper baselines (DeepSC, DASC, RL-ASC) implemented
  + Real datasets loaded (SST2, TTS audio, CIFAR-10 images)
